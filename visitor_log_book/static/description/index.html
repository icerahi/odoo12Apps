Visitor Log Management

Resolve the manual process for keeping, maintaining the employees and visitor’s information.

by Metamorphosis Ltd.

Overview

Any organization need an efficient method in order to handle the visitor's and employee’s information. For build strong business profile and smooth their business process, management need to record each and every detail of visitors and also record their visits. Logbook resolve automate their manual process for keeping, maintaining the employees and visitor’s information.

Features

  • Manage visitors
    • ↳ In that features responsible employee/staff can keep the record of visitor’s information like visitor’s name, contact information etc.

  • Check-in, Check out Details of Visitors.
    • ↳ The module can record visitors actual time when they entered and leave in the office

  • Manage department wise employee record
    • ↳ The user admin of this module can records department wise employees’ information

  • Manage Appointment Schedule
    • ↳ Employee can manage an appointment schedule that who wants visit in upcoming days in the office.

  • Visitors List
    • ↳ Admin can check the record of visitors list in visitor option

  • Multiple filters/group
    • ↳ Easy to find data by using filters & group in every option
